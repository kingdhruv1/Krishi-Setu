About KrishiSetu


KrishiSetu is a next-generation platform designed to transform the landscape of contract farming. By seamlessly connecting farmers, buyers, and companies, KrishiSetu ensures transparency, efficiency, and fairness in agricultural trade. With an emphasis on digital contracts, AI-driven farming recommendations, and direct market access, the platform empowers farmers to get the best deals for their produce while minimizing dependency on middlemen.

KrishiSetu introduces a holistic ecosystem where farmers can not only secure profitable contracts but also gain insights into optimizing their farm yields, pre-sell their crops, access financial assistance, and collaborate with fellow farmers for cooperative farming. The platform also features an e-commerce marketplace where surplus produce can be sold directly to consumers.

Through blockchain-enabled contracts, escrow-backed payments, and smart AI services, KrishiSetu is here to revolutionize the agricultural economy—making farming smarter, safer, and more sustainable for every stakeholder.

Our vision is simple: empower farmers, strengthen communities, and create a future where agriculture flourishes with innovation at its heart. KrishiSetu is not just a platform; it’s a bridge to a better tomorrow for everyone involved in the agriculture industry.

Our Prototype

Advanced Unique features

  • AI-Driven Crop Recommendations:

    Utilize AI algorithms that analyze soil quality, weather data, and market demand to suggest the best crops for farmers to grow. This ensures farmers select the most profitable and sustainable crops, increasing contract value.

  • Dynamic Contract Templates:

    Offering customizable contract templates for different types of farming agreements (e.g., organic farming, poultry farming, dairy, etc.) to ensure flexibility. Legal compliance tools will be integrated for easier contract creation and management.

  • Predictive Yield and Demand Forecasting:

    Useing AI and machine learning to predict crop yields and upcoming market demand. This information helps buyers plan purchases in advance and allows farmers to better negotiate contracts.

  • Blockchain-Based Contract Management:

    Securing all contracts using blockchain technology to ensure transparency, immutability, and trust between farmers and buyers. This would ensure that the contracts can’t be altered once agreed upon.

  • Real-Time Supply Chain Tracking:

    Included GPS-based tracking for produce in transit, ensuring both buyers and farmers can monitor the status and delivery timeline of the goods. This increases trust and reliability for the buyers.

  • Payment Guarantee through Escrow:

    Integrating an escrow payment system where funds from buyers are held in escrow until the farmer fulfills the contract. This guarantees timely and secure payments, reducing risks for both parties.

  • Farmer Insurance Integration:

    Providing crop insurance options directly on the platform. Farmers can purchase insurance against natural calamities or crop failures, providing further financial security.

  • Bulk Input Purchasing Deals:

    The platform will negotiate bulk deals with suppliers of seeds, fertilizers, and equipment, offering discounts to farmers who purchase inputs collectively. This can reduce their upfront investment.
